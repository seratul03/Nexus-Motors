Our Story

Founded in 2022, Nexus Motors began with a simple yet ambitious vision: to create electric vehicles that don't compromise on performance, luxury, or sustainability.

Our founders, a team of automotive engineers and designers from leading global brands, came together with a shared passion for innovation. They believed that the transition to electric mobility shouldn't mean sacrificing the emotional connection between driver and machine.

Today, we're proud to have delivered over 15,000 vehicles to customers across 45 countries, with each model representing our commitment to engineering excellence and environmental responsibility.

Our Mission

To accelerate the world's transition to sustainable transportation by creating electric vehicles that inspire passion, deliver exceptional performance, and respect our planet.
